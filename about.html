📖 Our Journey

Five Years of Excellence

From its founding in 2020, JPIA UE Caloocan has grown from a small but passionate group of accounting students into a thriving chapter recognized across the region and nation.

Each administration has built upon the foundation of those who came before — carrying forward a tradition of excellence, service, and professional development that shapes future CPAs.

What We Stand For

🎯

Mission

The Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants - University of the East Caloocan Chapter adheres to legislate and uphold a culture of academic excellence in all ventures of public victory, establishing dynamic student involvement through effective activities towards competency and excellency, providing equal opportunity to members showcasing their talent and skills in different fields, and developing a strong sense of commitment in leadership.

🔭

Vision

As a private non-political organization, JPIA University of the East - Caloocan envisions to prepare and produce versatile individuals who are highly competitive, set to defy their limits for holistic and aesthetic development; creating a venue for them to flourish academically and non-academically; Igniting their flame of excellency towards multiple paths and to be the pillars of democratic and student-centered leadership.

⭐
