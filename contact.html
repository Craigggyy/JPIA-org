Membership

How to Become a JPIA Member

Joining is simple! Make sure you have the following ready and reach out to us through our social media pages to get started.

🪪

Student Number

Your official UE student number (e.g. 2023-12345) is required to verify your enrollment status.

👤

Full Name

Provide your complete name exactly as it appears on your school records or ID.

📧

UE Email Address

Your official UE email (lastname.firstname@ue.edu.ph) will be used for all official JPIA communications.

📚

Year Level & Section

Let us know your current year level and section (e.g. BSA 2-1) so we can connect you with the right representative.

🎓

Course / Program

Membership is open to students enrolled in BS Accountancy (BSA) or BS Management Accounting (BSMA).

📞

Contact Number

A valid mobile number so our VP for Membership can follow up with you about your application status.

Simple 3-Step Process

1
Message Us Online Send us a message on Facebook or Instagram with your details ready.
2
Submit Requirements Our VP for Membership will guide you through submitting the necessary information.
3
Welcome Onboarding Attend the orientation and officially become a proud JPIA UE Caloocan member!

Ready to Join? Reach Out to Us!

Message us on our official Facebook or Instagram page to start your membership application. Our team will respond and walk you through the process personally.
